Broadsheet

WILDFLOWERS PROTECTION LAW

Centered on Israel's 1964 Wildflowers Protection Law the broadsheet addressing extinction and preservation in different contexts - nature, culture, and personal life. Using the Wildflowers Protection Law as a metaphor, the project frames broader processes of loss and conservation, reflecting on how preservation is perceived as a human act. The design emphasizes the tension between what remains present and what is fading away, echoing the law's original intent to protect what was disappearing.

10 pages, 55*37 cm, indigo print, glue binding, hebrew, 2025
