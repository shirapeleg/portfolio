Book Covers

BLOCK

An imaginary publishing house called "Block". "Block" is a cooperative press dedicated to showcasing local art and literature from every neighborhood. Its mission is to amplify the unique voices and creative expressions that emerge from specific urban communities. The logotype plays on a visual language that echoes the streets and branching pathways of a neighborhood. The design of the book covers is purely typographic, using only a single color and its shades. This minimalist approach aims to capture the essence of the publishing house's aesthetic - simple, spontaneous, and handmade - evoking the feel of a collage.

13*20 cm, Collage and digital processing, indigo print, hebrew, 2024
