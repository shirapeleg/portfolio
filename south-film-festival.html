Branding

SOUTH INTERNATIONAL FILM FESTIVAL

Founded by the School of Audio and Visual Arts at Sapir Academic College, the festival underwent a significant transformation in 2025 following October 7th. My rebranding concept was developed to reflect the current social and political reality. The festival's core values ״doing the most with the least״ - informed every aspect of the visual identity. The branding I created is rooted in a trilingual typographic approach, giving equal prominence to Hebrew, Arabic, and English. Visually, the identity draws on a raw, local aesthetic, using a minimalistic technique of black and orange spray paint. This choice reflects both the local nature of the festival and the creative use of space and materials - turning limitations into a design language that is economical, local and simple.

Spray Paint, Adobe Software, 2025
